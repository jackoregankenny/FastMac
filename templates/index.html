Dependency Management

When you select a tool, its dependencies are automatically selected. When you deselect a dependency, dependent tools are automatically deselected. Look for the "Depends" tag to see which tools have dependencies.

Privacy & Security

All installation happens locally on your machine. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted. Your selections are processed entirely in your browser.
